ABOUT US:

I started HIJABSTORE with my motivation to elevate the experience of hijab for everyone with a simple vision to have quality with affordability. It's difficult to find quality hijabs at affordable prices and variety for different occasions and seasons. At HeadsUp Hijab, We took it upon ourselves to source high quality hijabs without the ridiculous price tags for women from all walks of life. When I started to wear hijab for faith then I realise the incredible power Muslim women hold. In the face of challenges plaguing our world today, we consider it an honor to serve the Muslim woman by providing her with products, meaningful experiences, and resources to improve her relationship with hijab, expand her mind, enrich her life and nourish her soul.
